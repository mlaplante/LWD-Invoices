What changed in this redesign
A walkthrough of the specific UI improvements shown above
  • 1
    Typography system: Replaced Geist Sans with Instrument Serif for display text (KPI values, greeting, headings) and DM Sans for body — creates contrast between editorial warmth and clean utility. Numbers use DM Mono for tabular alignment.
  • 2
    Color palette shift: Moved from indigo-purple to warm amber (#c17b3a) as the brand primary, and swapped the flat white sidebar for a rich dark charcoal — matching the "Pancake" name's warmth. Background stays a warm off-white, not cold gray.
  • 3
    KPI cards with sparklines: The current 4-stat grid has no trend data. Added inline SVG sparklines + trend badges (↑ +18%) so the numbers have temporal context at a glance. Hover lifts the card to signal interactivity.
  • 4
    Cash flow widget: A new circular progress ring shows % of monthly invoiced revenue collected, alongside invoiced / collected / expenses / net. Currently missing from the dashboard entirely.
  • 5
    Activity feed: A chronological feed of meaningful events (payment received, invoice viewed, invoice created) replaces the static "recently viewed" list — more informative and conversational.
  • 6
    Invoice table refinements: Invoice numbers in monospace, semantic status dot inside each badge, amount column right-aligned in monospace — small details that make financial data easier to scan.
  • 7
    Sidebar navigation: Section labels ("Main", "Analytics"), a glowing active indicator dot, and a structured org pill at the bottom — cleaner information hierarchy without extra visual noise.
