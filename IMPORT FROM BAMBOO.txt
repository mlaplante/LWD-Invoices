How to import data from Bamboo Invoices
=======================================

1. Download http://i.28hours.org/files/pancake_export.php.
2. Place it in bamboo_system_files/application/controllers/pancake_export.php, in your Bamboo installation.
3. Go to your-bamboo-installation-url/index.php/pancake_export. Click the link to download your export file.
4. Go to your Pancake, into Settings > Import / Export, and import the file you got in the previous step. Leave the "What are you importing?" as "Invoices".
5. That's it! Click "Import Now!" and everything will be imported into your Pancake without any further input from you.
